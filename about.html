About Our Story

Nurturing nature since 2010, one plant at a time.

Our Mission

Greenery for Every Home

At GreenLeaf Nursery, we believe that every space, no matter how small, deserves a touch of nature. Our journey started in a small backyard with a handful of saplings and a vision to make sustainable gardening accessible to everyone in India.

We specialize in plants that are not just beautiful but are specifically chosen to thrive in the diverse Indian climate. From the humid coastal regions to the dry heat of the plains, we have a plant for every corner.

Why Choose Us?

We go beyond just selling plants; we offer a lifestyle of sustainability and health.

Quality Assured

Every plant is hand-picked and checked for health before it reaches you.

Eco-Friendly

We use sustainable potting mixes and eco-friendly packaging for all our plants.

Expert Guidance

New to gardening? Our experts provide lifetime care support for your green friends.
